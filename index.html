About

Hello, my name is Justin Madonna. I am from northern New Jersey, currently a senior in high school and will be attending the University of Rhode Island this fall. Over the past few years, I have gained a lot of experience with drone photography and have accumulated over nine hours of flight time on my newest drone. I have gotten interested in doing real estate photography after taking pictures of some of my family members houses. Currently, I am using a DJI drone, one of top the leaders in drone technology. The 12-megapixel images come out crystal clear and 1080P HD videos are liquid smooth thanks to the three-axis gimbal. I look forward to doing work with you. Please feel free to contact me below.
